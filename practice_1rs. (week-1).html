BMI Calculator



The BMI, Body Mass Index, is a measure of body fat obtainable by calculations using an individual's height and weight.

This is significant in the medical field as growing health conditions of obesity and malnourishment and enable the population of the nation is healthy.
As such, doctors usually ask patients with chest or joint problems for their BMI to know how to fix the problem; be it a diet or the gym.
Maintaining a healthy weieght is important to the heart health.
However, please note that the BMI is an imperfect measure of healthy body weight.
